A Little About Us

Here at Pet Charities & Projects, we are committed to finding loving, safe homes for all pets in need. What makes us different is that we have affiliates, which are growing everyday, who use our capabilities to not only adopt one or two animals. We turn these into large scale projects that you can be a part of today! Our sole agenda is to save the lives of starving, mistreated, ill, lonely animals that deserve happy, healthy homes full of love and kindness.

  • We can help you choose the best veterinarian in your area, as well as assisting you if you can no longer care for a pet.
  • We can make sure any pet you adopt will arrive to your home safely and from anywhere.
  • We provide free training videos on how to be a truly great pet owner and teach commands for great behavior.
  • All proceeds and much-needed donations are sent to the charity, or charities, of your choice or even to a specific pet.
  • We are involved in hosting fundraisers to gather other fellow foundations and always make a huge impact.
  • With an account, you can manage your own pet charity projects and make all the difference in the world to pets all over.
